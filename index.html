Hello, my name is Daniel. I'm a Computer Science major and a Music Composer. Ever since I was a child, I always had an interest in technology and music. It all started from playing video games and trying to imagine how the developers created the world I played in. How did they make the character move in such a way? How did they make the music? What exact process took them from scratch to such a complex program? It is the questions of how, what, when , where and why that got me here to this point in my career.
